Opportunities


This page lists all the different oppurtunites, connections, and resources that hacKSU is affliated with. Don't be afraid to reach out and talk to some of the companies/people to see what connections you can make.


  • Blackstone LaunchPad - Kent State University

    Blackstone LaunchPad is an innovative program that treats entrepreneurship as a viable career path. The program teaches students, faculty and alumni how to transform their ideas into thriving businesses.

  • LaunchHouse

    LaunchHouse is a lean business accelerator that fosters entrepreneurial success and job creation through investing cash, hard work and collaboration with interesting, like-minded people. Our mission is to create a hub of entrepreneurial innovation for promising startups and to help transform ideas into successful companies.

  • Cynergies Solutions

    Cynergies Solutions Group has multiple openings for entry-level to junior level candidates for a Software Developer opportunity with one of our clients located in Downtown Cleveland, OH. This is a great opportunity for those looking to learn and grown their talents in programming and coding. The Software Developer will have the chance to work as in a challenging, team environment focused on developing new projects and improving existing projects. Ideal candidates will have 1 year of relevant work experience or hands-on coding experience in an internship.
